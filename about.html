Keep calm and living on:

Data Multi-experience of the Human Stampede


Time: 2018/06/26


There has been long history about the stampedes, from time immemorial, man has been a gregarious animal, attracted by group activities naturally, which will lead the large crowd gatherings, even the stampedes in the end, which continue to happen frequently all over the world, and bring great loss to the humans. Under this situation, what threatens us is that we know little about the stampede, from how it happens to how to survive from it.

This project is divided into two main parts, first part is the data visualization of multi-aspects of the stampedes, which contains the public attitudes toward the stampedes and the analysis of the the main events, which contains 272 disasters from 1902 to 2018, which are divided into 8 main types (accident, cultural, entertainment, sport, religious, personal, political and terrorist), according to the article in Disaster Medicine and Public Health Preparedness, and many other subtypes ( like rumour, fire, football and so on ) for detailed analysis.

The second part is the data multi-experience of experimental solutions(like Human Microphone) to the public on a platform which aimed to raise the public attention through the multi-experience of the certain horrible event based on data to make the public aware of the dangers, and teach them the solutions to save themselves before the disasters occur.
